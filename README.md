## Problem Statement:

Managing vehicle spare parts, services, orders, and inventory manually is slow, error-prone, and inefficient. Suppliers face challenges in showcasing products and tracking orders, while customers and service centers struggle with checking availability and placing requests. This system provides a centralized mobile platform that improves efficiency, transparency, and reliability by enabling product browsing, ordering, service booking, and inventory management.
